CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

Join our professional team and help build the infrastructure of tomorrow.

Civil Engineers

We're looking for experienced civil engineers to join our team. You'll work on exciting projects including grading plans, stormwater management, and infrastructure design.

  • Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering
  • PE license preferred
  • Experience with AutoCAD and Civil 3D
  • Strong communication skills
Land Surveyors

Join our surveying team to provide accurate and professional land surveying services. Experience with modern surveying equipment and software required.

  • Licensed Land Surveyor preferred
  • Experience with GPS and total stations
  • Knowledge of surveying software
  • Attention to detail
Transportation Engineers

Help design and plan transportation infrastructure projects. Work on highways, roads, and transportation systems that connect communities.

  • Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering
  • Transportation design experience
  • Knowledge of traffic engineering
  • Project management skills
CAD Technicians

Support our engineering team with CAD drafting and design work. Create detailed drawings and plans for various projects.

  • Associate's degree or technical certification
  • Proficiency in AutoCAD
  • Experience with Civil 3D preferred
  • Strong technical skills
